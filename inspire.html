Rupaul

Rupaul is a fabulous queen who has brought light to the LGBTQ+ community. Rupaul has hosted many shows including Rupaul's Drag Race.

Not only that, but Rupaul made his drag show international, so now there is different franchises of the show. There is Rupauls Drag Race Downunder, Rupaul's Drag Race UK, and many more.

Throughout all these franchises, Rupaul and other judges have crowned about 44 queens who are changing the world. Recently, Rupaul crowned Jinkx Monsoon the queen of all queens.
